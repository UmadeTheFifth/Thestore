Our Story

The journey of theStore

2020

The Beginning

theStore was founded with a simple vision: to bring minimalist, high-quality fashion to everyone. Starting from a small studio in New York, we began curating our first collection of timeless pieces.

2021

Expansion

With overwhelming support from our customers, we expanded our product line to include watches, glasses, and accessories. Our commitment to quality and simplicity resonated with thousands of fashion enthusiasts worldwide.

2022

Going Global

We launched international shipping, making our products available to customers in over 50 countries. Our sustainable practices and ethical sourcing became a cornerstone of our brand identity.

2023

Innovation & Sustainability

We introduced our eco-friendly packaging and partnered with sustainable manufacturers. Our carbon-neutral shipping program was launched, reflecting our commitment to the environment.

2024

Community Growth

theStore community grew to over 100,000 satisfied customers. We opened our first pop-up stores in major cities and launched our rewards program to give back to our loyal customers.

2025

Today & Beyond

We continue to evolve, staying true to our core values of quality, simplicity, and sustainability. With exciting new collections and collaborations on the horizon, we're just getting started.
